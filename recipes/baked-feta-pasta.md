## Ingredients

- 6 oz block creamy Greek sheep/goat milk feta (not domestic — it'll come out grainy)
- 1 lb cherry tomatoes
- 4 cloves garlic, split
- 1/3 cup extra virgin olive oil
- Black pepper
- Crushed chile blend (Flatiron Pepper four pepper blend or similar)
- 8 oz pasta (half pound cooked)
- 1/4 cup reserved pasta water
- Fresh basil, chopped (lots)

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
2. Place feta block in the center of a casserole dish. Scatter cherry tomatoes and garlic around it.
3. Drizzle everything with olive oil, season with black pepper and crushed chile blend.
4. Bake until caramelized, 30-40 minutes.
5. Meanwhile, cook pasta and reserve some pasta water.
6. Add cooked pasta (half pound or more) and about 1/4 cup pasta water to the baking dish.
7. Stir it all together until the sauce emulsifies and turns creamy.
8. Top with lots of chopped fresh basil.

## Notes

- Must use creamy Greek sheep/goat milk feta — domestic feta is too dry and will turn grainy.
- Add more pasta if you prefer a higher pasta-to-sauce ratio.
